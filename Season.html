Season Story

Naruto ke is season mein, hamare hero ne naye challenges ka samna kiya. Dosti, pratishodh aur himmat ke saath, Naruto ne kai mushkil mod paar kiye. Har episode mein ek nayi kahani aur ek nayi seekh chhupi hai.

Is season ki kahani mein, Naruto aur uske dost ek saath milkar apne dushmanon se lade aur apne sapno ko haqiqat mein badla. Yeh season aapko ek adbhut anubhav pradan karega!
