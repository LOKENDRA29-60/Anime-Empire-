Episode Story

Is episode mein, Naruto ne ek aisa mod chuna jahan usse apne astitva aur lakshya ke beech ke rishte ka ehsaas hua. Har pal ek nayi chunauti lekar aata hai, jo Naruto ko aur majboot banata hai.

Episode ki kahani mein, dosti aur balidan ka ek anokha sangam hai jo aapko mantramugdh kar dega. Yeh episode aapke dil ko chhoo lega aur aapko ek alag hi anubhav dega.
